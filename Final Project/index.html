Home

Here at Alma's Cake Shop, we bake only the finest cakes in the valley, from strawberry cakes, chocolate cakes, to even Tres Leches cakes. Any type of cake that your heart desires, we can make a reality!

Looking for a chocolate cake? We got you covered! Craving a vanilla strawberry cake? Or just a Strawberry cake? Leave it to us! Want to order multiple cakes for a party (or just for yourself, we don't judge)? We can do it in no time!

More of the 'stay at home' type of person? Don't worry! By clicking on the 'Cakes' tab, you'll be able to see all of our delicous cakes while you are in your PJs!
